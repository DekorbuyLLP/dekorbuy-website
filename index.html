The North East India Story

Where Every Cane Tells a Story of the Hills

Deep in the valleys of Meghalaya, where bamboo grows thicker than anywhere else in the world, master craftsmen have been weaving cane furniture for generations. This is where Dekorbuy begins.

"We don't just make furniture. We make the forests of Northeast India walk into your most beautiful spaces."

Every piece in our collection is handcrafted by artisan families who have inherited their craft from grandparents. When you furnish your resort or homestay with Dekorbuy, you're not just buying furniture — you're carrying forward a living cultural tradition.

Our Story

Born in the Hills of Meghalaya. Built for India's Finest Spaces.

Dekorbuy Handicraft & Handlooms LLP was founded with a single conviction: that the extraordinary cane and bamboo craft traditions of North East India deserved a national stage.

We connect the skilled artisan families of Meghalaya directly with India's finest resorts, homestays, boutique hotels, cafes, and wellness retreats — creating value at both ends, and beauty in between.

🎋

Mission & Vision

What Drives Everything We Do

🎯

Our Mission

To bring the authentic handcraft legacy of North East India into the mainstream of Indian interior design and hospitality — creating dignified, sustainable livelihoods for artisan communities while delivering exceptional quality to our clients.

🌿

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted name in natural material furniture — where every buyer knows that Dekorbuy means handcrafted, sustainable, and authentic North East India.

The Human Story

Meet the Hands Behind Every Piece

Behind every cane chair and bamboo table is an artisan family with generations of knowledge and pride in their craft.

👨‍🎨
Master Cane Weaver

Bijoy Nongkynrih

East Khasi Hills, Meghalaya

Third-generation cane weaver with 28 years of experience. Bijoy specialises in the Khasi lattice weave pattern — a technique he learned from his grandfather at age 11. Today he leads a workshop of 12 artisans.

👩‍🎨
Bamboo Frame Specialist

Meena Marak

South Garo Hills, Meghalaya

Meena is one of the few women master craftspeople in Garo Hills. Her bamboo frame joinery is renowned for its precision — each joint hand-fitted without nails. She runs a women's craft collective of 18 families.

🧑‍🎨
Finishing & Lacquer Expert

Dhananjoy Boro

Ri Bhoi District, Meghalaya

Dhananjoy has developed natural lacquer formulas using traditional resin techniques that give Dekorbuy furniture its signature long-lasting finish. His treatment process extends furniture life by over 40%.

The Craft Journey

From Forest to Your Space

01
🌿

Sustainable Harvest

Cane and bamboo are hand-selected from sustainably managed forests. Only mature stalks above 3 years are harvested to ensure forest health.

02
🔨

Artisan Crafting

Master artisans shape, bend, and weave each piece by hand using traditional techniques passed down across generations. No factory machinery.

03
✨

Quality Finishing

12-point quality inspection. Natural lacquer or varnish finish applied in multiple coats for durability in commercial use environments.

04
🚚

Secure Delivery

Furniture is carefully wrapped and packed for long-distance transit. Delivered safely to your property anywhere across India.
